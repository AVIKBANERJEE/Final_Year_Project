PREDICTION OF ROAD ACCIDENT

Road accident prediction refers to the use of data analytics and machine learning techniques to analyze historical road accident data and predict the likelihood of future accidents. This can help improve road safety by identifying high-risk areas and enabling preventative measures to be put in place. It involves gathering and analyzing a range of data, such as weather conditions, road infrastructure, and driver behavior, to identify potential risk factors and patterns that can be used to forecast future accidents.
